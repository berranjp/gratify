Connect with your Community

Gratify is a new app designed to make you feel more connected with your local community. With Gratify, you can easily find the local businesses, artists, and more that make your community, a community!

Explore what's around you

See which businesses are near you using the interactive map. Gratify uses geolocation to only show you businesses in your immediate area.

See the highlights

Gratify highlights a new business every few weeks, you can always find something new to try!

Connect

View posts from businesses to see what’s going on, leave comments and reviews, and even chat direcly with local business owners!

Give thanks

Share the love! Let others know what you love about your favorite places.

Make meaningful relationships

As a business owner, Gratify can help you make meaningful relationships with your customers by giving you a direct connection with them.

Gratify gives you the freedom to customize your business’ page, make posts, and chat directly with both new and returning customers.
